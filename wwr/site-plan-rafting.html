Overview

Purpose

Rafting is for many people a once in a lifetime experience. For others, it's a staple of their outdoor experience. It is exhilarating and awe inspiring. And regardless of your experience level, we want to be your destination for rafting. The purpose of this web site is to inform, educate and engage potential customers.

Audience

Our audience is both the experienced and the newbie customer. We specialize in team building events.
